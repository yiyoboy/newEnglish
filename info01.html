A long,very long time ago,rabbits had long,very long tails.
One day,a long-tailed radbbit came outside to play.

I.Pic Description:

  • What can you see in this picture?
  • Words suggested: field, bush, jump.

II.Words you must learn:

  1. long [lɒŋ] adj. 长的；

    1. long ~
    2. [exercise]: making sentences with ‘long’ X 5

  2. time [taɪm] n. 时间 [Note]: 不可数名词，变为复数时为 time

    1. long time no see.
    2. What is the time?
    3. What time is it?
    4. What time did you get up?
    5. What time did she come?
    6. What time are you going to go to school?
    7. What time are we going to go to our English class?
    8. What time do you go to bed every day?
    9. What time does he brush his teeth every day?
    10. What time should I start doing my homework?
    11. [exercise]: making sentences with ‘time’ X 5

  3. ago [ə'gəʊ] adv. 以前；

    1. I went to her house two days ago.
    2. The teacher came 5 minutes ago.
    3. I watch this movie 1 week ago.
    4. She went to this school 1 year ago.
    5. He was a baby 3 years ago.
    6. It was a little dog 3 months ago.
    7. I finished reading this book two classes ago.
    8. We started learning English a year ago.
    9. What time did she call? She called you 1 hour ago.
    10. What time did the class start? The class started 10 minutes ago.

  4. rabbit ['ræbɪt] n. 兔子。

    1. l[Note]: 可数名词，变为复数时为 rabbits

  5. tail [teɪl] n. 尾巴

    1. [Note]: 可数名词，变为复数时为 tails
    2. [To think about]: What animals have tails?

  6. outside ['aʊtsaɪd] n. 外面, 外界, 外表
    [Note]: 反义词 inside

    1. The children are playing outside.
    2. Please go outside to smoke.
    3. Come outside and play with us.
    4. I can’t go outside, I have a lot of （许多）homework to do.
    5. Do you want to put the rubbish outside?
    6. There is a car outside our house.
    7. Why does the dog sleep outside the house?
    8. Did you play with your friends outside today?
    9. What is the special sound outside?
    10. Let’s sit outside and drink some coffee.
    11. [exercise]: making sentences with ‘outside’ X 5

  7. came [keɪm] v. come的过去式；来，到来，到达

  8. play [pleɪ] v. 玩

    1. 和。。。。。。玩：play with + 人
    2. 玩+物品：play computer; play toys; play seesaw; play rope-jumping (rope-skipping);
