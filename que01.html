A long,very long time ago,rabbits had long,very long tails. One day,a long-tailed radbbit came outside to play. 查看翻译 很久很久以前，兔子有很长的尾巴。 一天，一个长尾巴的兔子去外边玩。

I.Pic Description:

  • What can you see in this picture? 查看翻译 你能在图片中看到什么？
  • Words suggested: field, bush, jump. 查看翻译 field：固定 ，bush：灌木， jump:跳

II.Words you must learn:

  1. long [lɒŋ] adj. 长的；

    1. long ~
    2. [exercise]: making sentences with ‘long’ X 5

  2. time [taɪm] n. 时间
    [Note]: 不可数名词，变为复数时为 time

    1. long time no see. 查看翻译 好久不见
    2. What is the time? 查看翻译 几点了？
    3. What time is it? 查看翻译 几点了
    4. What time did you get up? 查看翻译 你几点起床？（get up 起床）
    5. What time did she come? 查看翻译 她几点来的？（come 来）
    6. What time are you going to go to school? 查看翻译 你将要几点去学校？（is going to 将要 ）
    7. What time are we going to go to our English class? 查看翻译 我们将要几点上英语课？（are going to 将要 ）
    8. What time do you go to bed every day? 查看翻译 你每天几点睡觉？（go to bed 将要 ）
    9. What time does he brush his teeth every day? 查看翻译 他每天几点刷牙？（brush teeth 将要 ）
    10. What time should I start doing my homework? 查看翻译 我应该几点开始做我的作业？（doing my homework 做作业 ）
    11. [exercise]: making sentences with ‘time’ X 5

  3. ago [ə'gəʊ] adv. 以前；

    1. I went to her house two days ago.查看翻译 我两天前去的他家（two days ago 两天前 ）
    2. The teacher came 5 minutes ago.查看翻译 老师5分前来的（5 minutes ago 5分钟前 ）
    3. I watch this movie 1 week ago. 查看翻译 我一周前看的这个电影？（watch movie 看电影 ）
    4. She went to this school 1 year ago. 查看翻译 她一年前来的这个学校（went to this school 来这个学校， went （go 的过去式） ）
    5. He was a baby 3 years ago. 查看翻译 他三年还是个婴儿（ was ，is 的过去式 ）
    6. It was a little dog 3 months ago. 查看翻译 它3个月前还是个小狗（3 months ago 3个月前 ）
    7. I finished reading this book two classes ago. 查看翻译 我两节课前读完这本书（finished reading book 读完书）
    8. We started learning English a year ago. 查看翻译 我们一年前开始学的英语（started learning English 开始学英语 ）
    9. What time did she call? She called you 1 hour ago. 查看翻译 她几点打的电话？ 她一个小时前给你打的电话（call you 给你打电话 ）
    10. What time did the class start? The class started 10 minutes ago.查看翻译 几点上的课？10分钟前开始上的课。（10 minutes 10分钟 ）

  4. rabbit ['ræbɪt] n. 兔子。

    1. l[Note]: 可数名词，变为复数时为 rabbits

  5. tail [teɪl] n. 尾巴

    1. [Note]: 可数名词，变为复数时为 tails
    2. [To think about]: What animals have tails? 查看翻译 什么动物有尾巴？（animals 动物）

  6. outside ['aʊtsaɪd] n. 外面, 外界, 外表
    [Note]: 反义词 inside

    1. The children are playing outside. 查看翻译 孩子们正在在外边玩（are playing 正在玩）
    2. Please go outside to smoke. 查看翻译 请去外边吸烟（go outside 去外边）
    3. Come outside and play with us. 查看翻译 出来外边和我们玩(come outside出来外边)
    4. I can’t go outside, I have a lot of （许多）homework to do. 查看翻译 我不能去外边，我有很多作业要做（go outside 去外边）
    5. Do you want to put the rubbish outside? 查看翻译 你想要把垃圾放外边么？（put the rubbish outside 把垃圾放外边）
    6. There is a car outside our house. 查看翻译 我们家外边有一辆车（there is 有）
    7. Why does the dog sleep outside the house? 查看翻译 为什么狗睡在家外边（sleep outside 外边睡）
    8. Did you play with your friends outside today? 查看翻译 你今天和你的朋友在外边玩了么？（play *** outside 和***在外边玩）
    9. What is the special sound outside? 查看翻译 外边是什么特殊的声音？
    10. Let’s sit outside and drink some coffee. 查看翻译 我们去外边坐下来喝点咖啡吧！（sit outside 外边做）
    11. [exercise]: making sentences with ‘outside’ X 5

  7. came [keɪm] v. come的过去式；来，到来，到达

  8. play [pleɪ] v. 玩

    1. 和。。。。。。玩：play with + 人
    2. 玩+物品：play computer; play toys; play seesaw; play rope-jumping (rope-skipping);
